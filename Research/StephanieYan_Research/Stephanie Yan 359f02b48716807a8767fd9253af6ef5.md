## user stories

**User Story:**

As a student developer working on a team project, I want a centralized platform to manage tasks, communicate progress, and stay aware of my teammates’ availability so that I can collaborate more efficiently and avoid missing important updates.

**Scenario / Workflow:**

1. The user logs into the platform and views their sprint dashboard.
2. They see assigned tasks, deadlines, and current progress.
3. The user updates their status (e.g., *Coding*, *In Class*, or *Need Help*).
4. They submit a quick standup update describing completed work, upcoming tasks, and blockers.
5. The platform automatically summarizes project updates and highlights important issues.
6. The user receives only relevant notifications for subscribed task categories.
7. Throughout the sprint, they monitor progress and collaborate with teammates without information overload.
